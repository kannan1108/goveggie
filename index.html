Chicken is not
chicken.

The chicken in a fast food sandwich and a pasture-raised, antibiotic-free bird are not the same food. Same name. Different substance entirely.

Factory farming uses growth hormones to double the size of a bird in half the time. Antibiotics to keep sick animals alive in conditions that would otherwise kill them. Then we eat those antibiotics. Then we wonder why they stop working.

This isn't about being vegetarian. It's about knowing what you're actually eating. And choosing differently — one meal at a time.
